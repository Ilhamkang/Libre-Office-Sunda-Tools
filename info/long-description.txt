This oxt extension package for Libreoffice provides dictionaries for spelling and hyphenation for the Sundanese language of Indonesia.

The files su_ID.dic, su_ID.aff and hyphen_su_ID.dic were modified by Ilham Nurwansah (ilhamnurwansah@gmail.com) based on the Indonesian version created by Benitius Brevoort (benitius.brevoort@kapusin.org or benitius@tiscali.it)
